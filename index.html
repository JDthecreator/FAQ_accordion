FAQs
What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor is totally free! If you care to challenge yourself with more advanced projects we do offer paid content such as real figma designs and computing resources.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my Portfolio?

Of course you can! You can modify these projects anyway you like!

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

Please feel free to visit our Solutions page where you can ask for help from a group of your peers. Many of which have already completed the same project enabling them to give you incomparable insight.
